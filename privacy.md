 # Privacy Policy — NorthPass Play

  **Last updated: March 2026**

  NorthPass Play is developed by NorthPass VFX.

  ## Data Collection

  NorthPass Play does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  - All media files, playlists, and annotations are stored **locally on your 
    device only**
  - We do not use analytics, crash reporting, or tracking of any kind
  - We do not share any information with third parties
  - We have no access to your files, media content, or usage behaviour
  - No account or login is required to use the app

  ## Third-Party Services

  NorthPass Play uses no third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising
  networks.

  ## File Access

  The app accesses media files you explicitly select via the Files app or
  Photos picker. Files are never uploaded or transmitted anywhere.

  ## Changes to This Policy

  If our privacy practices change, we will update this page with a new date.
